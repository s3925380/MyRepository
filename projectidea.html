Project Idea

Overview:

Tired of having to use different applications to access different financial accounts? This app works to combine all your bank accounts, all your bills, all your loans, your savings, and your superannuation, while assuring you are a 100% safe, 100% secure and with 100% privacy guarantee as you use the app. According to U.S.News there are concerns that most finance apps have high prices, synchronisation issues and privacy concerns in public settings (Williams, 2021). The goal is to make this app easy to use, 100% private, 100% secure, affordable for everyone, and have more reliability towards live information synchronisation.

Motivation:

This app is the go-to app for managing and forecasting all your financial needs through automated graphs, algorithms displaying repeated payments for example, previously paid electric bills, gas bills, or water bills to estimate the next upcoming bills, a more live streamed update of customer finances and maintaining the privacy and security of consumer information. Statistics show a high trend cycle in countries such as United States, Australia, and India, according to google trends (Apps, 2020).

Description:

This application focuses on the upcoming expenses through using automation and algorithms, to detect previous billing or income of expenses paid, upcoming bills and saved income, to help the consumer identify unnecessary spending habits, essential spending requirements, bill tracking, and visual aid based on daily activities. You could also set a personal spending limit which can help you get notified if you are overspending, before reaching that limit.

The features include:

  • • Available on PC and Smartphones or Tablets
  • • Smart phones and tablets synchronise with PC application
  • • Connecting with the consumers bank provider to give a live display of income and expenses through the app
  • • Connecting with billers such as, Phone and Internet providers, Electricity, Water, Gas and Land Rates to finalise payments your bills and show the next upcoming due bill date
  • • Automated graphs to visually display expenses versus income
  • • Easy navigation to all billing and financial platforms
  • • 100% confidentiality of consumer information and data
  • • 100% protection with daily updated security measures (Fraud and scam detection, hacker prevention and anti-virus programming in built to the application)
  • • FAQ and chat support 24/7
  • • Scheduled payment services
  • • Reminders of upcoming events
  • • An automated alert system which displays a warning that the customer maybe overspending and provides notification to the customer on all smart device and through email
  • • Has a feature to link accounts with Tax Authorities, which will help the small business owners to manage their taxable income more efficiently
  • • Can be used for personal and/ or business use
  • • Easy access to expert financial advice for a fee
    •

    With the consent provided by the consumer, banking institution and billing provider, our app will combine their information into our secure and private servers, which can be accessed only by our customers using the app, and with given protection by our cyber security team with reassurance of the idea that everyone’s information is staying confidential and safe 24/7.

    The automated programming will consist of data collection of certain information, like account balances, bill balances, income balances, non-essential spending (anything considered not to be an income or bill), automatic alert notifications and due dates to display that information on our app over a 24-hour period.

    With a fee to provide our services, this will help protect consumer information and accounts, provide budgeting advice from real financial experts, schedule, or place reminders of upcoming expenses, and provide automated graphs showing consumer spending and savings every day, all in one app.

    Outcome:

    When the application is completed successfully, the consumer using our product and services will have the guarantee of security from hackers, fraud or scammers and viruses, the ability to identify their financial history, the viewing of their upcoming bills, viewing of saved income all in one place, use of our many finance calculators and most importantly making the app free, considering that a consumer can also seek financial advice from a professional using our services. This resolves the problem by separating the applications functionality direct to the cyber world, through our own servers to keep our guarantee of our keeping our customers information confidential and secure.
